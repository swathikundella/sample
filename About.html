Who We Are

Zero Cost Hiring is a Project Development cum Hiring Company. At ZCH, highly experienced professionals assist you in job placement, which helps refine your soft skills that are integral for interviews. Our team offers training in technologies such as UI Design, Application Development, Digital Marketing, etc.

ZCH is not a consulting company, rather an institute with a staff augmentation approach that specializes in placing candidates with a previous experience or none, in any field.

Until now, we have successfully placed around 1000+ candidates in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai & Pune, and will continue to do so. In a nutshell, no matter what you have done previously, rest assured, you will always find a job at ZCH.

What do we do?

  • Zero Cost Hiring has a tie-up with multiple IT Companies, thus expanding your reach.
  • A team of honest, skilled and experienced IT trainers, recruitment managers, placement executives who are always ready to support and guide you on all aspects pertaining to training and placement activities.
  • Zero Cost Hiring acts as a perfect bridge between students, professionals and companies/employers.
  • We focus and work with SME’s, MNC’s and CMMI companies, on specific requirements as and when needed by them.
